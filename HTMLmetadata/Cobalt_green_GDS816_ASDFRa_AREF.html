SPECTROSCOPIC_DISCUSSION:

This pigment has a reflectance peak centered at 0.534 microns with an composite absorption centered at 0.66 microns composed of overlapping absorptions centered at 0.580, 0.627,and 0.698 microns with a weak shoulder at 0.756 microns. There is also an even broader composite absorption centered at 1.56 microns composed of overlapping absorptions with a shoulder at 1.083 microns. A broad absorption dominates the 2 micron region and is centered at 2.253 microns (continuum removed positions). Positions of absorptions and number of absorptions in composite absorptions with overlapping bands are approximate, and would require spectral deconvolution to determine the actual number and position of the absorptions. G. Swayze

END_SPECTROSCOPIC_DISCUSSION.
