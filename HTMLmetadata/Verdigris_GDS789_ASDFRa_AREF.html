SPECTROSCOPIC_DISCUSSION:

This pigment has a strong reflectance peak centered at 0.493 microns and a broad absorption that stretches from 0.49 to 1.4 microns centered at 0.755 microns. Numerous vibrational absorptions dominate the NIR portion of the spectrum. Individual absorptions are centered at: 1.457, 1.492, 1.557, 1.582, 1.666, 1.683, 1.722, 1.748, 1.773, 1.861, 1.946, 1.990, 2.192, 2.252, 2.284, 2.310, 2.403, and about 2.47 microns (continuum removed positions). G. Swayze

END_SPECTROSCOPIC_DISCUSSION.
