SAMPLE_DESCRIPTION:

The sample appears uniform dark green and the visible-NIR spectrum shows strong iron bands and a spectrum typical of pure annite. The sample is darker than WS660 and the absorption features near 2.3-µm are weaker, possibly indicating a more iron rich end-member than WS660.

XRD_ANALYSIS:

Trioctahedral mica - major - probably a mixture of compositions. No other components.

Konnert, Judith and Marta Flohr, 1992, unpublished data, USGS Reston, VA.

40 kV - 30 mA, 7.3-9.5 keV
File: annit661.out, -.mdi (smear mount on quartz plate)
References: Huebner's reference patterns
Found: trioctahedral mica, 3T or 1M polytype, un indexed weak reflections at 8.8 and 4.24 Angstroms

Comment: Non-basal reflections are surprisingly weak, even considering the smear mount. Some peak broadening or splitting, as in WS-660. Based on the position of the (060) reflection, could not distinguish between siderophyllite (60-100%) - annite solution or annite (70-90%) - phlogopite solution.

J.S. Huebner, J. Pickrell, T. Schaefer, written communication(USGS)
