Spectra from 2.5 to 25 µm for this sample are published in:

Salisbury, J. W., Walter, L. W., and Vergo, N., 1987, Mid-Infrared (2.1-25µm) Spectra of Minerals: First Edition, U.S. Geological Survey Open File Report 87-263.

"Sample is translucent and light gray to white in color. This is the classic Amelia albite. Hand specimen is large (2 cm) fragment of twinned crystal. Appears very fresh. Is translucent and light gray to white in color."

Sample measured for the library is the 74-250µm interval.
