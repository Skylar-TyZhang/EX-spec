SPECTROSCOPIC_DISCUSSION:

Spectrum has absorptions characteristic of all mixture phases to varying degrees. Jarosite has its typical Fe-absorptions in visible out to 1.2 microns with a weak band at 2.2664 micron. Gypsum has it triplet in the 1.4 - 1.5 micron region and muscovite has its absorption at 2.2092 microns.

END_SPECTROSCOPIC_DISCUSSION.
