SAMPLE_DESCRIPTION:

Green chlorite-serpentine bearing skarn rock at contact between dolomite and quartz monzonite intrusive at the Bessemer Mine (iron [magnetite]).
Mixture of Clinochlore (Phyllosilicate-Chlorite group) and Lizardite, Antigorite, and Orthochrysotile (Phyllosilicates-Kaolinite-Serpentine Group).

SPECTROSCOPIC_DISCUSSION:

Broad one micron Fe+2 absorption band, with a 1.4 micron chlorite & serpentine feature. The two micron features show a mix of chlorite and serpentine, with a strong 2.32 (OH) feature, a weak 2.25 shoulder (slightly chloritic, mainly serpentine) and a weak 2.38 micron band (lesser serpentine, moderately chloritic)
