SAMPLE_DESCRIPTION:

Theoretical percentages of the oxides in the formula are BaO 65.7%, SO3 34.3%. Sr substitutes for Ba and a complete solid solution series extends to celestite, but most material is near one end or the other of the series. A small amount of Pb may also substitute for Ba.

Sieve interval 74-250µm.
