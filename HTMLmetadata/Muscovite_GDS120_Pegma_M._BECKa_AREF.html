References: Bailey (1984, p. 574), Huebner's reference patterns
Found: dioctahedral mica, 2M1 polytype
Sought but not found: quartz, (100) would be distinct if present

Comments: Very similar patterns. Only mica detected. The mica (004) reflection is at 17.6-17.9 degrees 2 theta, indicating a large alkali such as potassium (the dioctahedral Na-mica, paragonite, has the (004) reflection at 18.4 degrees). The small sample size required smear mounts, which are responsible for a strong preferred basal orientation in each of the patterns."

J.S. Huebner, J. Pickrell, and J. Randow, 1993, written communication.
