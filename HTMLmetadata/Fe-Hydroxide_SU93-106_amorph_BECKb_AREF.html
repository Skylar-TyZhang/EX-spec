SPECTROSCOPIC_DISCUSSION:

Spectrum shows a strong absorption edge in the visible with a weak absorption at 0.9637 microns. This band position is compatible with goethites and schwertmannite. Lack of a band near 0.65 microns may indicate the sample is possibly schwertmannite or nanocrystalline goethite or both.

END_SPECTROSCOPIC_DISCUSSION.
