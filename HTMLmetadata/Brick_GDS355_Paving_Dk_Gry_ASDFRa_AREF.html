SPECTROSCOPIC_DISCUSSION:

The strong absorption edge centered about 0.57 microns gives this paver its dark gray color. The spectrum decreases monotonically from 0.8 microns into the near infrared. A weak absorption around 2.2 microns may indicate the presence of mica or clay. G. Swayze.
