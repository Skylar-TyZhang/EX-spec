SPECTROSCOPIC_DISCUSSION:

This pigment has a broad reflectance peak centered at 0.44 microns with a weak absorption at 0.481 and a stronger broader absorption centered at 0.588 microns. Other broad bands are centered at 1.36 (many overlapping absorptions) and 2.164 microns (continuum removed positions). Positions of absorptions and number of absorptions in absorption complexes with overlapping bands are approximate, and would require spectral deconvolution to determine the actual number and position of absorptions. G. Swayze
