SAMPLE_DESCRIPTION:

Sample is a Chlorite - Muscovite phyllite from the Lower Cambrian Harkless Formation collected west of hwy 95 from south of the western relict hydrothermal center. This sample is from outside of the alteration zone and is representative of the regionally metamorphosed Harkless Formation.

SPECTROSCOPIC_DISCUSSION:

Spectrum is dominated by Fe-related Chlorite absorptions in the electronic portion of the spectrum (.2 - 1.35 microns) and dominated by both muscovite and chlorite absorptions in the vibrational region (1.35 - 2.5 microns). There is a Al-OH combination band from Al-rich muscovite at 2.1970 microns. Bands at 2.2596 and 2.3461 microns are due to chlorite.

END_SPECTROSCOPIC_DISCUSSION.
