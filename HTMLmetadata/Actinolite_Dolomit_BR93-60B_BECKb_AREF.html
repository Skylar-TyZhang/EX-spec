SPECTROSCOPIC_DISCUSSION:

Actinolite surface coating on calcite. Broad generic Fe+2 one micron absorption feature with strong 1.39, 2.31, and 2.39 actinolite features.
The sharp 1.39 micron OH feature (with the 2.3 features) identifies actinolite.
The minor minerals identified with XRD may slightly influence the actinolite spectrum.

END_SPECTROSCOPIC_DISCUSSION.
